Featured Artist
Watercolor & Ink

Yuki Tanaka

Yuki Tanaka is a Tokyo-based artist known for her delicate watercolor and ink paintings that blend traditional Japanese techniques with contemporary themes. Her work often explores the relationship between nature and urban life.

Oil Painting

Hiroshi Yamamoto

Hiroshi Yamamoto has been creating stunning oil paintings for over 20 years. His work is characterized by bold colors and dramatic seascapes that capture the power and serenity of the ocean. His paintings have been exhibited in galleries across Japan and internationally.

Abstract & Mixed Media

Mei Nakamura

Mei Nakamura is an emerging talent in the world of abstract art. Her mixed media creations combine acrylics, textiles, and found objects to create vibrant, textured pieces that invite viewers to explore their own emotional responses. Her work has been featured in several group exhibitions for emerging artists.
